# GRC Glossary

## GRC

Governance, Risk, and Compliance. A structured approach to managing organizational policies, risks, controls, and regulatory obligations.

## Control

A safeguard or process designed to reduce risk.

## Risk

The possibility that a threat or weakness could negatively impact the organization.

## Control Mapping

The process of linking policies, procedures, or requirements to framework controls.

## Gap Analysis

A review that identifies missing, weak, or incomplete controls.

## Audit Evidence

Documentation or proof used to show a control operated effectively.

## Control Owner

The person or team responsible for maintaining and providing evidence for a control.
