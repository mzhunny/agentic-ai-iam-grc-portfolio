# Agentic AI Security Glossary

## Agentic AI

AI systems that can plan, reason, use tools, and take actions toward a goal.

## AI Agent

A system that can interpret instructions, interact with tools, and complete multi-step tasks.

## Human-in-the-Loop

A control where a person must review or approve important actions before they are completed.

## Prompt Injection

An attack where malicious instructions attempt to manipulate an AI system into ignoring rules or leaking data.

## Tool Permission

The level of access an AI agent has to external systems, APIs, files, or applications.

## Audit Logging

The recording of system actions to support monitoring, investigation, and compliance.

## Guardrail

A technical or procedural control designed to keep AI behavior within approved boundaries.
