# IAM Glossary

## IAM

Identity and Access Management. The discipline of managing digital identities and controlling access to systems, applications, and data.

## RBAC

Role-Based Access Control. A model where access is assigned based on job roles.

## SoD

Segregation of Duties. A control that prevents one user from having conflicting responsibilities or permissions.

## JML

Joiner-Mover-Leaver. The identity lifecycle for onboarding, role changes, and offboarding.

## Access Review

A periodic review of user access to confirm permissions remain appropriate.

## Least Privilege

The principle that users should only have the minimum access needed to perform their job duties.

## Privileged Access

Elevated access that allows users to perform administrative or sensitive actions.
